Fiskasupa

Description

Given their expertise in fishing, itâ€™s no surprise that fish was a common Viking meal.
This hearty stew would warm them on cold nights.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs mixed fish
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 4 cups fish or vegetable broth
  • 2 cups diced root vegetables
  • 1 cup leeks, sliced
  • 1 tsp dill
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large pot, saute the onion and leeks until translucent.
  2. Add the root vegetables and cook for another 5 minutes.
  3. Pour in the broth and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and let simmer until vegetables are tender.
  4. Add the fish and dill. Cook for another 10 minutes or until the fish is cooked through.
Enjoy this delicious stew with a slice of flatbread.
