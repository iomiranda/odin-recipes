Jasmine Green Milk Tea

Description

This Jasmine Milk Tea recipe is made using Jasmine green tea, a tea which is bold in flavor and perfectly pairs with the creaminess of the milk.

The result is a sweet, smooth, and delicate drink with floral perfumed notes.

Ingredients

  • 2 jasmine green tea bags
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • ice, to serve

Steps

  1. Boil the water in the kettle and place the jasmine green tea bags into a jug.
  2. Once boiling, pour the water into the jug and steep the tea for 2 minutes.
  3. Then remove the tea bags and discard. While the tea is hot, stir in the honey to dissolve.
  4. Set the jasmine green tea aside to cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Then into a tall glass add ice and milk, pour over the jasmine green tea and enjoy the chilled drink.
