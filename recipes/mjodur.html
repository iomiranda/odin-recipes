Mjodur

Description

No Viking feast would be complete without mead, the classic honey-based alcoholic drink.

Ingredients

  • 1 gallon water
  • 1.5 lbs honey
  • 1 packet yeast

Steps

  1. In a large pot, heat water and honey until well combined. Do not boil.
  2. Cool the mixture to room temperature, then add yeast.
  3. Pour into a fermentation container, seal, and allow to ferment for at least six weeks before bottling.
Drink up!
