Tennessee Tea

Description

One of my favorites! Similar to a Long Island, but with Jack Daniel's!

Ingredients

  • 2 fluid ounces whiskey (such as Jack Daniel'sÂ®), or to taste
  • 1 fluid ounce triple sec
  • 1 fluid ounce sweet and sour mix
  • 1 fluid ounce cola, or to taste

Steps

  1. Pour the whiskey, triple sec, sweet and sour mix, and cola into a cocktail shaker over ice.
  2. Cover, and shake until the outside of the shaker has frosted.
  3. Strain into a chilled glass over ice to serve.
