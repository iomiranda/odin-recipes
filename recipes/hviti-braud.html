Hviti Braud

Description

Flatbreads were a staple in the Viking diet due to their simple ingredients and quick cooking process.

These could be made from barley, oats, or rye.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rye flour
  • 1 cup barley flour
  • Water (enough to form a dough)
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Mix the flours and salt in a large bowl.
  2. Gradually add water until the mixture forms a firm dough.
  3. Divide the dough into small balls and flatten them into discs
  4. Cook over a dry skillet or hot stone until each side is golden brown
